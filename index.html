Honest advice
Should you invest in LinkedIn Sales Navigator?
🔍
Probably not yet — and here's why
₹11,000–₹12,000/month · Advanced outreach tool
LinkedIn Sales Navigator is a powerful tool — advanced filters, saved lead lists, InMail credits, CRM integration. But at ₹11,000–₹12,000 per month, it's a significant additional cost, and it's not necessary to get strong results in the first 90 days.

The strategy outlined here is built entirely on LinkedIn's free features plus a modest ad budget. The recommendation is to validate first — build the audience, land the first few clients, and prove the model works. Once leads are coming in consistently and the subscription pays for itself within one or two closed deals, then it becomes a straightforward investment. Start with the free trial if you're eligible, and measure whether it genuinely adds enough pipeline to justify the ongoing cost.
Hold off if…
  • Still building the first client base
  • Organic content strategy isn't proven yet
  • Free LinkedIn tools haven't been fully used
  • Monthly revenue doesn't absorb the cost easily
Worth it when…
  • Leads are coming in consistently
  • Actively scaling B2B outreach volume
  • Need niche filters free search can't provide
  • 1–2 closed clients cover the monthly cost
